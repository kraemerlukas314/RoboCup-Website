Hi, I'm Simon from team Krypton. Team Krypton was founded in 2022 although I have build a few simple linefollowers with a Lego Mindstorms Ev3 since 2018.
We first participated RoboCup in Febrary 2023, you can find all four runs on the EvB Robocup YouTube channel.
Unfortunately we only got 3rd place in St. Augistin so we didn't qualify for German Open in Kassel.

For 2024 we try to use a Raspberry Pi in combination with a Pi camera to improve our linefollowing and picking up the blue rescue kit.
